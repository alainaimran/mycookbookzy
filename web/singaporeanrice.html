Singaporean Rice Recipe

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 1 whole chicken (about 3-4 lbs)
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 4-5 slices of ginger
  • 3-4 stalks of green onions
  • Water (enough to submerge the chicken)

For the Rice:

  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 3 cups chicken broth (reserved from poaching the chicken)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4-5 slices of ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt (to taste)

For the Chili Sauce:

  • 2-3 red chilies (deseeded for less heat)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Salt (to taste)

For the Ginger Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons ginger (grated)
  • 2 tablespoons green onions (finely chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt (to taste)

Instructions

Preparing the Chicken:

  1. Clean the Chicken: Rub the chicken with salt, both inside and out, and rinse thoroughly.
  2. Poach the Chicken: Place the chicken with ginger slices and green onions in a large pot. Cover with water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for about 30-40 minutes until cooked.
  3. Cool the Chicken: Remove the chicken and let it cool. Reserve the broth for cooking the rice.

Preparing the Rice:

  1. Rinse the Rice: Wash the jasmine rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain well.
  2. Sauté Aromatics: Heat vegetable oil in a pot, add ginger slices and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Cook the Rice: Add the rinsed rice, stir, then pour in the reserved chicken broth, add salt, and bring to a boil. Cover and simmer on low heat for 15-20 minutes until fluffy.

Preparing the Sauces:

  1. Chili Sauce: Blend the red chilies, garlic, sugar, lime juice, and salt until smooth. Adjust seasoning as needed.
  2. Ginger Sauce: Mix grated ginger, chopped green onions, vegetable oil, and salt in a small bowl.

Serving:

  1. Carve the Chicken: Once cooled, chop the chicken into pieces.
  2. Plate: Serve the rice on a plate, arrange the chicken pieces on top, and drizzle with ginger sauce.
  3. Accompaniments: Serve with chili sauce on the side, along with sliced cucumbers for garnish.
