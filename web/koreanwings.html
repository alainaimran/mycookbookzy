Korean Chicken Wings Recipe

Ingredients

For the Chicken Wings:

  • 2 pounds chicken wings
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Sesame seeds and chopped green onions, for garnish

For the Marinade:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 2 tablespoons honey or brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar or apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for extra heat)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Chicken Wings

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400째F (200째C).
  2. Season the Wings: In a large bowl, toss the chicken wings with vegetable oil, salt, and pepper until evenly coated.
  3. Bake the Wings: Spread the wings in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake in the preheated oven for 40-45 minutes, flipping halfway through, until they are crispy and golden brown.

2. Make the Marinade

  1. Combine Ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together soy sauce, gochujang, honey (or brown sugar), rice vinegar, minced garlic, minced ginger, sesame oil, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using) until well combined.

3. Glaze the Wings

  1. Coat the Wings: Once the wings are cooked, remove them from the oven and place them in a large bowl. Pour the marinade over the hot wings and toss until they are evenly coated.
  2. Bake Again: Return the coated wings to the baking sheet and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, allowing the glaze to caramelize and adhere to the wings.

4. Serve

  1. Garnish and Enjoy: Remove the wings from the oven and sprinkle with sesame seeds and chopped green onions. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce or alongside pickled vegetables.

Tips

  • Frying Option: For extra crispy wings, you can deep-fry them instead of baking. Fry the wings at 350째F (175째C) until golden brown, then toss in the marinade.
  • Make-Ahead: You can marinate the wings in the sauce for a few hours or overnight for more flavor.
  • Serving Suggestions: These wings pair well with rice, salads, or as part of a larger spread of appetizers.
